Variational Quantum Eigensolver for Kagome lattice spin liquids

Variational methods are employed to approximate the ground states of each atom in a molecule to gauge its chemical properties. I am working on creating an implementation to of VQE with a reduced the time complexity of approximating the ground state for antiferromagnetic spin liquids in a Kagome lattice.

Error Correction Analysis of Quantum Circuits

Currently, no quantum computer has a method of ensuring accuracy when measuring the collapsed state of a qubit due to quantum entanglement. I constructed a variety of randomly arranged quantum circuits where the expected qubit configuration probabilites are calculated and compared to the measured results on IBM's quantum backend.

Measuring Distances with Ultrasonic Pulses (2023)

High frequency sound waves are commonly used to measure the distance between two points in many applications such as robotics. By isoltating only high frequency sound waves that an ultrasonic receiver may utilize, we can effectively measure the distance by the time it takes to emit and retrieve a pulse.

Workflow Priorities

I have six key priorities that I follow with my work.

Precision

Every major iteration or design I create must exceed the specified project deliverables and must be ready for any failure modes.

Organization

Every item in the project must follow a consistent and communicatable form of etiquette as well as proper docummentation.

Continual Improvement

Between every iteration or design update, there must be progress between each update of the component to maintain development.

Efficiency

Given a deadline to complete an objective, the workflow must be easy to track and manage to avoid potential problems in the future.

Troubleshooting

When encountering some type of failure, it is important implement many hypotheses and testing metrics of what caused the issue to learn from the mistake.

Communication

It is important to maintain a clear, respectful, and effective method of communication to propel project logistics.
